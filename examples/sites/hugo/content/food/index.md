## After Coffee

Grab your cup and walk. In New York, the {{< alap "@nyc_parks" >}}parks{{< /alap >}} are
steps from most coffee shops. In SF, take your Blue Bottle to
{{< alap "dolores" >}}Dolores Park{{< /alap >}} and watch the city go by.
