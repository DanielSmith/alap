# Packages

Projects in the Alap ecosystem that consume the `alap` library as a dependency. These live in their own repositories and demonstrate what you can build on top of Alap's expression engine, renderers, and protocol system.

## alap-gather

Drag menu items into a collection tray to build ad-hoc sets from multiple Alap menus. Gather combines static config entries with live protocol feeds, organizes them into folders, and exports to JSON, Markdown, or browser bookmarks.

**HighNotes** — double-click or drag a collected item out of the tray to materialize a floating card on the page. Cards are draggable, pinnable to the viewport or anchored to scroll position, collapsible, and z-stacked. Tag chips on cards open a filtered lightbox scoped to that tag across all collected items.

In development. Source: [github.com/DanielSmith/alap-gather](https://github.com/DanielSmith/alap-gather)
